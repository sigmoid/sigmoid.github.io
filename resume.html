PRCO (Team of 2):

Please Respond, Colony One is an in-progress adventure game where players paint the memories of an astronaut. I Implemented all of the game's systems including vehicle controls using Unity's physics, and multiple custom shaders to render stylized paintings of what the player sees, including an implementation of a kuwahara filter which animates the painting process.

Project Duration: October 2016 - present.

Engine: Unity

Contribution: Programming, Design, Writing

Retrostasis(Team of 4):

2D puzzle platformer. I made the original prototype which won 4th place in the Gamejolt GDC jam, then went on to work on it with a team. I wrote a custom SAT collision detection and resolution system, and custom physics to achieve a desired gamefeel. I also wrote a camera script to follow the player while framing important obstacles.

Project Duration: 4 months

Engine: Unity

Contribution: Programming, Design




Attractor(Team of 4):

A unique bullet-hell game where the player attracts enemy bullets. I wrote C++ scripts for the player's movement, enemy AI, and bullet gravitation.
